Predictive Analytics

Predicting the future may sound impossible, but it is no longer a far fetched reality. Advances in machine learning enables us to actually provide accurate predictions based on historical data. Through various models of machine learning algorithms, historical data and factors associated with an outcome can be used as a foundation for the computer to determine probable future values.

The use cases of predictive analytics are endless. Want to know which customer will respond to your email marketing campaign? Want to determine when your supply might be disrupted? What about the ability to determine, in advance, if a new product would be well received or not? Would knowing a probable spending amount for a user on a given day be helpful to your business? Simply using your historical data, machine learning engines can find a pattern and apply models to report a possible result of your business decisions, all before you actually act on them.
